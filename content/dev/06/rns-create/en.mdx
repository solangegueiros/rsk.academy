## What is RNS - RIF Name Service?

RNS is an open source project which provides an architecture to enable the identification of blockchain addresses by human-readable names.

The concept of a name resolution service facilitate de use of addresses for reaching the unbanked and non-technical users. 

- In Mainnet, acquiring a domain cost less than a dollar. 
- Once a domain is acquired, subdomains can be delegated with no additional cost.
